Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

To address local and global challenges, Bangladesh 2.0 is implementing modern technology and innovations in every sectors of the country.

Sustainability

Bangladesh 2.0 is aiming to balance economic growth with environmental preservations through sustainable development and emphasizing green practices

Infrastructure

From transportation to digital network, modern infrastructure projects are transforming Bangladesh.

Empowerment

Bangladesh 2.0 is empowering its individuals and communities through education, healthcare and social programs.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

When school children will be on a tour of the poverty museum, they will be horrified to see the misery and indignity of human beings. They will blame their forefathers for tolerating this inhuman condition to continue in a massive way.”

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
