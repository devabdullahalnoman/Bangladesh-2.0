Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

To address local and global challenges, Bangladesh 2.0 is implementing modern technology and innovations in every sectors of the country.

Sustainability

Bangladesh 2.0 is aiming to balance economic growth with environmental preservations through sustainable development and emphasizing green practices

Infrastructure

From transportation to digital network, modern infrastructure projects are transforming Bangladesh.

Empowerment

Bangladesh 2.0 is empowering its individuals and communities through education, healthcare and social programs.
